# CMDAI – FAQ

### Does CMDAI work offline?
Yes, 100% offline.

### How do I add a model?
Place any .gguf file inside the `models/` folder.

### Does CMDAI work on Windows?
Yes, Windows is the primary platform.

### Can I use multiple models?
Multi‑model You can't use yet.

### Does CMDAI have an API?
Yes, available endpoints:
- /generate
- /chat
- /pull
- /tags
- /version

### Why CMDAI?
CMDAI is built for people who want an AI tool that feels truly their own.  
No cloud, no accounts, no tracking — just fast, private, local AI running directly in your terminal.

It gives you full control, instant performance, and a clean developer‑friendly workflow.  
If you want an AI experience that is simple, powerful, and completely yours, CMDAI is the right choice.
